  MAXIMUM gravity field.
  Everyone tracks the queen.
  Where the queen IS =
  where attention GOES.

  The queen IS the nuke player.
  25 kill streak.
  Everyone's orbit bends
  around the queen.

KING: 8 squares.
  Tiny gravity.
  But INFINITE importance.
  The singularity.
  If this falls, game ends.
  Not because it's powerful.
  Because it's ESSENTIAL.

10^120 POSSIBLE GAMES.
But grandmasters don't calculate
10^120 positions.

They see FLOW.
They see ATTENTION.
They see where the gravity IS
and where it's GOING.

IT'S ATTENTION MANIPULATION.

You're buying FLOW
with MASS.
